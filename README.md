# Gamecock WNBA Roll Call: 30th Season Edition 2026

**12 Gamecocks in the WNBA: Season 30 Roll Call**

An interactive D3 data visualization by Kris E. Smith (@theblacktechie) commemorating 12 South Carolina Gamecocks on WNBA opening day rosters for the league's 30th season.

Published May 7, 2026.

---

## What It Does

An animated roll call that reveals each of the 12 Gamecocks in the order they entered the WNBA, from Allisha Gray (2017) through Madina Okot (2026). A live counter tracks progress from 0 to 12 alongside a garnet progress bar. Users can play through automatically, pause, step through manually by clicking any row, or reset and replay.

No build step. Open `gamecock-wnba-rollcall.html` directly in a browser or deploy to GitHub Pages as a static file.

## Context

South Carolina leads the nation in WNBA draft picks since 2020. The 2026 WNBA season is the league's 30th. Tyasha Harris signed with the Indiana Fever in April 2026, a homecoming to her native Noblesville, Indiana. Raven Johnson, Ta'Niya Latson, and Madina Okot are 2026 WNBA Draft selections.
